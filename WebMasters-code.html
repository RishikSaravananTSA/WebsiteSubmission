About Us

Urban Oasis is a place where plant-based indian cuisine meet city life. We go to the local market as often as possible, sourcing crops from small family-run farms, in an effort to reduce our footprint. We have created cozy and welcoming spaces where you can catch your breath, an oasis amidst the city's blaring noise. Come to Urban Oasis and discover how delicious and gratifying vegetarian dining can be--be it a lunch stop-in, dinner rendezvous, or lazy weekend brunch. Your choice will nourish your body and be kind to the planet as well. Welcome to your new favorite escape; every bite is a step toward a healthier, sustainable world.

Our Vision

We aim to be more than just a restaurant; we are an homage to seasonal food that celebrates fresh, local produce and dedicated eating. We have a special gimmick, being, there are no names of the items, you see which item looks the most appealing and check it out! Urban Oasis believes that one must nourish his body and soul.
